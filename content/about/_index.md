Currently, I’m an AI Engineer at Proofpoint, where I work on adapting LLMs to the email-security domain. I specialize in:

- MLOps/MLE/SWE: Building automated ETL pipelines with Flyte so models don’t just stay in notebooks.
- Data Engineering: Wrangling large-scale datasets with Amazon Athena and Polars to fuel the next training run.
